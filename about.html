About


Research Associate in the Cronin Group, University of Glasgow. Expert digital chemist with experience working on discovery projects in nanoscience, coordination, medicinal, and system chemistries. Skilled in a broad range of chemical synthesis (including air-sensitive), laboratory automation, and the application of data science and machine learning to chemical problems. Industrial experience as an Intern in Medicinal Chemistry at F. Hoffman-La Roche Ltd. Experience teaching complex concepts to students of all ages.
