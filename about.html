Daniel J. Kowalski, PhD

Expert Digital Chemist, with half a decade of experience as a research scientist in the application of machine learning and robotics to discovery in inorganic chemistry. Gained familiarity with the pharmaceuticals industry as a medicinal chemistry intern during my first degree.

Currently employed as an Assistant Language Teacher with the JET Programme. Working with students aged 5 to 15. Key interests in the theory of learning and pedagogy, and the introduction of new technologies into education.
