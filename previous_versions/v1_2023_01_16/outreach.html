Outreach, Teaching

Outreach Work

  • P7 School Outreach: (March 23rd 2022) Supervised two P7 classes (10-11 years old) in ink chromatography and footprint identification activities as part of a Forensics-themed workshop.

  • Pint of Science Festival, Local Organiser: (2019-20) Worked as part of a small team to deliver a three-night public engagement event at a local pub as part of a national festival. Organised speakers within the theme "Atoms to Galaxies" (encompassing physical sciences). Communicated with the venue. Planned additional activities for each evening. Ultimately cancelled due to Covid.

  • S6 School Outreach: (December 6th 2019) Supervised a group of Advanced Higher Chemistry students (16-18 years old) in the purification of crude benzoic acid by liquid-liquid separation and TLC.

Teaching

Mentoring / Tutoring

  • Project Lead: (Oct 2020 - Nov 2021) Supervised a full-time research Masters student during the course of a joint research project. Helped with the development of good laboratory practice, synthetic and presentation skills, and chemical understanding. Student went on to pass with minor corrections.

  • Academic Tutor: (Jan 2019 - May 2019) Paid tutor to a 1st year undergraduate, covering content on Thermodynamics, Chemical Kinetics, Aqueous Equilibria, Transition Metals.

Undergraduate Laboratory Demonstrating

  • Synthesis 1 Lab: (2 years) Experiments covered: (i) Extraction and Purification of Benzoic Acid, (ii) Synthesis of Paracetamol

  • Quantitative 1 Lab: (1 year) Experiments covered: Inorganic Pyrotechnics (introductory experiment with an emphasis on making detailed observations and safe laboratory practice)

  • Synthesis 2 Lab: (4 years) Experiments covered: (i) Preparation of triphenylphosphine oxide metal halide complexes, (ii) Preparation of cobalt and iron compounds, (iii) Synthesis of a Grignard Reagent, (iv) Aromatic Chemistry, (v) Acetylation of ferrocene
    As part of a small group of teaching staff, produced a series of videos to enable online teaching of the nucleophilic aromatic substitution experiment.

  • Quantitative 2 Lab: (1 year) Experiments covered: (i) HPLC, (ii) Flame Photometry

  • Inorganic 3 Lab: (2 years) Experiments covered: (i) Transition Metal Complexes, (ii) Hydrothermal Synthesis and Properties of Zeolites
    Individually produced a series of demonstration videos to enable online teaching of the hydrothermal synthesis of zeolites experiment.

Other Undergraduate Teaching

  • Chemistry 1 Workshops: (2 years) Workshops on Redox, Thermodynamics, Chemical Kinetics, and the Chemistry of the Carbonyl Group to first years.

  • 1st Year Support Groups: (1 year) Fortnightly sessions for first years online during Covid as a support system. Sessions acted as group tutoring sessions and a forum to raise any issues or worries during the pandemic.

  • Online Synthesis 1 Lab: (1 year) Online support for remote first year labs due to Covid.
