Digital Chemistry, Data Science, Discovery

I am an early career researcher working at the interface between chemistry and computer science. I use optimisation algorithms and bespoke metrics, in tandem with robotics, to explore chemical search spaces and try to determine new, more efficient ways to discover novel substances. This often requires the ability to interpret large and complex datasets and I use techniques from data science to help make sense of the chemistry happening on the bench.

Currently employed as a Research Associate in the Cronin Group, University of Glasgow. Open to new opportunities!
