About Me

Currently in the final year of a PhD in Digital Inorganic Chemistry with the Cronin Group, University of Glasgow. Industrial experience in F. Hoffmann-La Roche Ltd. Keen interest in education and outreach.

I received an integrated Masters degree from the University of Leeds in 2019, with a year as an industrial intern with F. Hoffmann-La Roche Ltd. (medicinal chemistry) and a Masters project in the McGowan Group (organometallic chemistry). Currently, I am in the final year of a doctorate with the Cronin Group at the University of Glasgow, with an expected completion date in Autumn 2022. My PhD focuses on the application of automation, machine learning, and data science techniques to nano- and coordination chemistry.
